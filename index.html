Students of Colour Collective

The UVSS Students of Colour Collective is a group of self-identified racialized students, (including Black, Indigenous, Asian, Latin American, Middle Eastern, and Pacific Islander communities, among others) operating as an advocacy group out of the University of Victoria, located on Xʷsepsəm, Songhees, and W̱SÁNEĆ territories.

SOCC serves all students who identify with a visible or invisible ethnic/cultural minority, and works to promote cultural diversity and build community on-campus and in the Greater Victoria Community.
